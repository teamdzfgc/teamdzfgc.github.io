Journey

On July 14th, the nine members who demonstrated outstanding performance during the FGC training were selected to be part of the FGC 2023 building team.

These individuals showcased remarkable dedication, discipline, and an insatiable eagerness to learn. Filled with joy and anticipation, these robotics enthusiasts embarked on this new chapter of their adventure.

Think it through

The initial step our team undertook was uniting our youthful and brilliant minds through over five intensive brainstorming sessions. During these sessions, we meticulously dissected the challenges, deliberated on our ideas, and formulated the necessary mechanisms.

Filled with enthusiasm and pride in representing Algeria, we sketched the blueprint of our envisioned robot. With unwavering determination, we prepared ourselves for the tasks of construction and coding.

Picture Perfect

Dressed in our traditional clothes , we embarked on a journey to the most exquisite historical site within our city “La Casbah “ . Each of us bore a puzzle piece featuring a distinct continent, symbolizing the harmony of humanity across the globe. This profound moment marked the inception of our team's visual narrative.

Much like how we, as teammates, achieve wholeness through mutual respect, benevolence, and effective communication, so too does the entirety of the world's population find unity and fulfillment in the interwoven fabric of our collective existence.

Connecting with the world:
Meeting with teams from
all over the globe

Undoubtedly, one of the most awe-inspiring aspects of this expedition was the opportunity to connect with fellow teams hailing from every corner of the globe, allowing us to delve into a myriad of cultures and nations.

Our encounters spanned continents, as we engaged with teams representing Africa, Asia, Europe, and the Americas. The privilege of such cross-cultural exchanges enriched us immeasurably, both as distinct individuals and as a cohesive team. The experience endowed us with empowerment and gratitude, crystallizing into a profound chapter of our journey.

Community Project

Embracing the role of an FGC (First Global Challenge) member transcends mere robot construction and coding; it embodies active participation within one's community and the act of contributing to its betterment.

Much like the acquisition of technical skills, fostering cooperation, embracing responsibility, and nurturing solidarity form integral parts of this journey. These lessons crystallized vividly during our involvement in a community initiative.

With this poster Our foray into the realm of community impact was exemplified by our collaboration with the "Association El Amal," dedicated to facilitating the healing of children with autism through equine therapy. Upon our arrival, we were warmly welcomed and guided through the premises, gaining insight into the therapeutic process. Subsequently, we immediately engaged in a hands-on demonstration of our commitment by undertaking a thorough cleaning of the facility. This gesture underscored our dedication to not only learning technical skills but also embodying the values of compassion, teamwork, and active engagement that define the essence of being an FGC member.

Inspiring next generations
in STEM: FGC team Algeria
Open Day

On this exceptionally memorable day, our hearts brimming with immense joy and delight, we extended a warm embrace to more than forty middle schoolers and high schoolers.

Our senses were captivated by the exuberant young minds, brimming with eagerness to delve into the captivating realm of robotics.

During this significant event, we shared our personal journey, weaving our experiences throughout the training and competition, recounting our entry into this domain, the lessons we absorbed, and the growth we underwent. Subsequently, we imparted to the students the art of constructing and programming a miniature robot.

As the day culminated, the radiant smiles that graced their faces upon witnessing their robots spring to life enabled us to perceive not merely students, but the potential future engineers and scientists of Algeria through their eyes.

Building The Robot

Having acquired the essential technical and soft skills through our training, and having confronted competitions and challenges that pushed us beyond our comfort zones, we transitioned from novices to proficient members endowed with substantial knowledge and experience. This marked the inception of our robotic journey.

Functioning as a cohesive unit, we collaboratively supported one another, acutely aware of the weight of our collective responsibility. Each team member dedicated relentless effort, pouring their heart and soul into the mission at hand. Our objective was to craft a singular robot—a fusion of mechanisms and systems capable of executing all the requisite tasks set forth by this year's challenge. This goal remained steadfast, even during the most trying and complex junctures, when the burdens felt overwhelming and our spirits wavered. It was during these exact moments that our united front proved invaluable. Amidst exhaustion and dwindling motivation, we rallied each other with a resounding reminder: "This is our dream."
