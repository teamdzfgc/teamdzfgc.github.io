Biographies

Acharf Meguellati, 26

Achraf Meguellati is a dynamic and dedicated Senior STEAM Education Specialist who has been shaping educational experiences at World Learning since 2018. With a strong commitment to fostering creativity and learning, Achraf leads by delivering innovative STEAM teacher training programs. Grounded in the principles of Universal Design for Learning, his approach ensures inclusivity and accessibility in education. Achraf's influence goes beyond the classroom as he takes on the role of overseeing the FGC Team Algeria since 2019. In this capacity, he guides and empowers young minds to explore the realms of Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics (STEAM), equipping them with skills crucial for their future success. His passion for education, combined with his strategic leadership, continues to inspire both educators and students to embrace the world of STEAM and its boundless possibilities.
